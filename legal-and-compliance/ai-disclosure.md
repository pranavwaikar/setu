# AI Content and Assistance Disclosure

**Last Updated: June 2026**

This document details the usage of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies within the Setu project development, generation of assets, and content.

---

## 1. AI-Assisted Development
*   **Code Generation:** Sections of the Setu codebase, styling frameworks, and documentation templates have been designed and written with the assistance of advanced AI coding systems (such as Google Gemini/Antigravity).
*   **Quality Assurance:** All AI-suggested code is reviewed, refactored, and tested by human maintainers to ensure correctness, compliance, and security before being merged into the official release branch.

## 2. No Automated User-Data Processing by AI
*   The public Setu gateway and CLI client do not route user data through external AI systems or Large Language Models.
*   Your request headers, request bodies, and database configurations remain strictly confidential and are never sent to third-party AI models for training or parsing.
