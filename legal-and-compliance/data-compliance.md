# Data Compliance Statement

**Last Updated: June 2026**

This document outlines the data compliance standards for the Setu tunneling service hosted by Helios Logic.

---

## 1. Compliance Disclaimer
Setu is an open-source development project and experimental demo. It is **not** designed or certified to comply with strict regulatory frameworks such as HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act), PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard), or SOC 2.

## 2. Platform Usage Limits
*   **No Sensitive Data:** Users must not transmit, process, or store sensitive personal information (SPI), personally identifiable information (PII) of a sensitive nature, financial records, credit card numbers, or medical data through the public Setu gateway.
*   **Developer Sandbox:** The gateway is designed for local development testing only. All network traffic routed through the platform is processed at the user's sole risk.

## 3. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) & California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
*   As the operator of the public demo, Helios Logic collects minimal operational data (emails, chosen subdomains, connection status).
*   Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to request deletion of your account and related metadata. Since we do not retain traffic contents, we possess no user traffic logs to access or delete.
*   To request complete removal of your registration records, please submit an issue or contact the administration team.

## 4. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
Setu is built for software developers, engineers, and IT professionals. We do not knowingly target or collect information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please notify us immediately for deletion.
