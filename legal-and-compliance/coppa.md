# COPPA Compliance Statement

**Last Updated: June 2026**

This document outlines compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for the Setu project and its public gateway demo.

---

## 1. Target Audience
*   **Developer and Professional Focus:** Setu is a command-line utility and web-based developer console built for software developers, engineers, system administrators, and IT students.
*   **Not Directed to Children:** The platform, its features, and documentation are not directed at, designed for, or intended to attract children under the age of 13.

## 2. No Collection of Children's Data
*   Helios Logic does not knowingly collect, request, or store personal information (such as names, emails, or physical addresses) from children under the age of 13.
*   Registration requires user confirmation of age. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that information from our servers.

## 3. Contact Us
If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has registered for an account or submitted personal information to Setu, please contact the administrators immediately to initiate account removal.
